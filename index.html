Game Rules 📋

  • Objective: Guide your cat through increasingly difficult levels to reach the goal door.
  • Controls: Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move left/right and jump.
  • Platforms: Jump across platforms to reach higher areas and avoid falling.
  • Enemies: Ground enemies walk back and forth, flying enemies hover and bob in the sky. Touching any enemy costs a life.
  • Lives: You start with 3 lives. Lose all 3 and it's game over! Each new level resets your lives to 3.
  • Scoring: Earn 100 points for each level you complete. Try to get the highest score!
  • Difficulty: Each level gets progressively harder with more platforms, enemies, and flying threats.
  • Goal: Reach the golden door at the end of each level to advance.
